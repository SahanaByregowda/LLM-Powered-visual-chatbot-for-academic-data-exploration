# Student’s Mobility Part

The EUMaster4HPC students need to be enrolled in two different awarding universities for Year 1 and Year 2 of the master’s programme. Eligible students get a scholarship to facilitate this mobility.

As a mandatory part of the programme, the mobility path also includes participation in EUMaster4HPC extracurricular activities, such as workshops, internships, or summer schools.

Please take into consideration that some universities have certain prerequisites. Before choosing any kind of mobility, you will always have to ensure that you have confirmation from both your Study Programme Director.

## Awarding Universities for Mobility

The following universities are involved in the student mobility program:

* Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg
* Sorbonne Université
* Université du Luxembourg
* Università della Svizzera Italiana
* Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya
* Kungliga Tekniska Högskolan
* Politecnico di Milano
* Sofia University St. Kliment Ohridski
