# ETP4HPC Conference 2024, 13-14 February, Sassenheim, The Netherlands

News from Wednesday 31 January 2024

Students from EUMaster4HPC have been kindly invited as special guests to the ETP4HPC Conference 2024, offering them a prime opportunity to actively participate, gain insights, and establish impactful connections during this upcoming event.

The ETP4HPC Conference 2024 offers an exciting chance for students enrolled in EUMaster4HPC, to enhance their professional network and build valuable connections within the European High-Performance Computing community.

Amandine Samson, EUMaster4HPC project manager at Sorbonne University, will be allocated a speaking slot to present the EUMaster4HPC project to the audience.

Taking place on the 13th and 14th of February in the charming town of Sassenheim near Amsterdam, this second edition of the ETP4HPC Conference promises to be an engaging experience.

Find out more about the ETP4HPC Conference 2024 on www.etp4hpc.eu
