# EUMaster4HPC participation to ISC High Performance 2023

News from Wednesday 17 May 2023

EUMaster4HPC will participate to the ISC High Performance 2023, which will take place in Hamburg, Germany from 21 to 25 May 2023.

ISC is the largest forum in Europe for HPC, high performance data analytics and AI/machine learning. This annual event brings together the international HPC community, providers and users from industry, public institutions and research organisations.

Save the dates of the EUMaster4HPC presentations and visit us to discover more about the programme and discuss win-win partnership:
* Monday 22 May 2023 from 16.00 to 16.30
* Tuesday 23 May 2023 from 12.30 to 13.00
* Wednesday 24 May 2023 from 15.15 to 15.45

## ISC practical information

* **When:** Sunday 21 May 2023, 08:00 CEST - Thursday 25 May 2023, 18:00 CEST
* **Where:** Congress Center Hamburg, Germany
* **Language:** English
