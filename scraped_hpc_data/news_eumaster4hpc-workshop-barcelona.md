Workshop: Career Day and BSC Quantic Group Presentation, 23 February 2024, UPC Barcelona
News from Tuesday 16 January 2024
Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya Campus Nord c/ Jordi Girona 1-3 08034 Barcelona
The next EUMaster4HPC workshop will take place at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC) in Barcelona on Friday 23 February 2024. Students from the first pan-European master dedicated to the High Performance Computing will attend to outstanding career talks as well as the BSC Quantic Group presention.

This semester
EUMaster4HPC
workshop will take place at the
Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya
(UPC) in
Barcelona
on
Friday
23 February 2024
.
Students from the first pan-European master dedicated to the
High Performance Computing
will attend to
outstanding career
talks, with speakers from companies, supercomputing centers and institutions such as
Google
,
Intel
, the
Barcelona Supercomputing Center
(BSC) or the
École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne.
Also, the
BSC Quantic Group
, whose research focuses on the application of Quantum computers to hard scientific problems, will be presented to the audience.
This workshop is offered in
hybrid format
and students will be able to take part
in person or online
.
Hosted by
Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya
,
Euro HPC JU
and
seven prestigious european universities
, this event promises
smart
and
excellent
discussions.
