EuroHPC Summit 2024, 18-21 March 2024, Antwerpen, Belgium
News from Tuesday 27 February 2024
As last year, the students of the EUMaster4HPC programme are invited to actively participate in the EuroHPC Summit 2024, which will take place in Antwerp, Belgium, from 18 to 21 March 2024.
Join EUMaster4HPC at the EuroHPC Summit 2024!
Event Highlights
Date
18-21 March 2024
Location
A Room with a Zoo, Antwerpen, Belgium
Theme
Exascale and Beyond, Unleashing the Power of European HPC and Quantum Computing
The four-day summit will revolve around the theme of
Exascale and Beyond
, delving into the potential of
European High-Performance Computing
(HPC) and
Quantum Computing
.
Demo Lab
Immerse yourself in the future of computing at the Demo Lab, featuring operational supercomputers from the
EuroHPC Joint Undertaking
. Managed by representatives from each supercomputing centre and 26 EUMaster4HPC Student Ambassadors, this interactive space offers a unique opportunity to witness and explore the capabilities of these cutting-edge supercomputers.

For more information and registration, visit the
